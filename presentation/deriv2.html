Solution 1

\(\forall x \in \mathbb{R},\) \(\quad f(x) = 4x + 7\)

\(f'(x) = 4\)

Solution 2

\(\forall x \in \mathbb{R},\) \(\quad f(x) = x^2\)

\(f'(x) = 2x\)

Solution 3

\(\forall x \in \mathbb{R},\) \(\quad f(x) = -x^2 + 11x + 3\)

\(f'(x) = -2x + 11\)

Solution 4

\(\forall x > 0\) \(\quad f(x) = \sqrt{x}\)

\(f'(x) = \frac{1}{2\sqrt{x}}\)

Solution 5

\(\forall x \in \mathbb{R},\) \(\quad f(x) = 6 - 2x\)

\(f'(x) = -2\)

Solution 6

\((u\times v)' = u'v + v'u\)

Solution 7

Solution 8

\(f'(x) = 30x^2+2x+9\)

Solution 9

\(\forall x \in \mathbb{R}^*,\) \(\quad f(x) = \frac{1}{x}\)

\(f'(x) = -\frac{1}{x^2}\)

Solution 10

\(\forall x \in \mathbb{R},\) \(\quad f(x) = \frac{2}{3}\)

\(f'(x) = 0\)
